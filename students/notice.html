Discipline

  1. I-Card

    Wearing I-card is compulsory, if a student is found without I-card, then a fine of Rs.50/- is levied for that day.

  2. Attendance
    • 75% attendance is compulsory.
    • No-attendance, if students are late by @10 min. in any class/Lab, but s/he will be allowed to be seated and attend that class/Lab.
  3. Leave
    • More than 3 days of leave, needs documented evidence.
    • Prior intimation for leaves is must.
  4. Official Leaves
    • For extracurricular activities, with prior permissions.

CLASSROOM INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Wearing I–card is compulsory.
  2. Switch-OFF cell phones inside classroom during lectures hours otherwise it will be confiscated.
  3. Students are required to wear semi-formals when they attend the institute
  4. Maintain Silence during lecture hours.
  5. Students will be entitled for no-attendance if they are late inside the classroom after 10 min. of commencement of lecture.
  6. Any damage to the classroom property by the students will have to be compensated by the concerned students.
  7. Eating inside the classroom is prohibited.
  8. Keep the classroom clean.
  9. Substance abuse is strictly prohibited in the campus.
  10. Switch-off fans and lights when not in use.
  11. Collect your valuables before leaving the classroom

LABORATORY INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Wearing I–card is compulsory.
  2. Bags & water bottles must be kept outside the laboratory.
  3. Abide by Safety Rules and Instructions.
  4. Handle all instruments with care and responsibility.
  5. Students will be entitled for no-attendance if they are late inside the laboratory after 10 min. of commencement of practical.
  6. Switch-OFF cell phones the laboratory during practical hours otherwise it will be confiscated.
  7. Maintain Discipline during practical.
  8. Report any breakage to the Lab-in-charge.
  9. Any damage to the Laboratory property by the students will have to be compensated by the concerned students.
  10. Return all equipment before leaving the laboratory.
  11. Switch-off main power supply when not in use.
  12. Collect your valuable before leaving the laboratory.
